About Us

Project Finder was a project created by a PC enthusiast by the name or Robin. In his frustration of there being only a select few trustworthy Computer and Computer Vendors, Project Finder was born. Its aim being to be a store trusted by the community that aims to provide Computers and Computer components at competetive prices, while also ensuring that from the moment a customer presses order to when the product arrives at their doorstep is hasstle free. We aim to provide nothing under exeptional service to all out customers.
