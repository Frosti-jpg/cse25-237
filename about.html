About Us


Built by enthusiasts, for enthusiasts.


Project-Finder was created from a simple idea: getting quality PC hardware and reliable prebuilt computers shouldn't be difficult.


Before our store began, finding trusted PC components, gaming hardware, and professionally built systems locally wasn't always easy. Limited options, inconsistent availability, and a lack of dedicated PC-focused businesses inspired Robin to create something different - a place where builders, gamers, students, creators, and everyday users could find the tech they need under one roof.


Whether you're assembling your dream gaming rig from scratch, upgrading an older system, or looking for a powerful ready-to-go prebuilt PC, we've got you covered.


We stock a growing selection of PC parts, gaming accessories, components, and custom/prebuilt systems, chosen with performance, reliability, and value in mind. But beyond the hardware, we're building a community around a shared passion for PCs and technology.


We know the excitement of choosing parts, cable-managing a fresh build, hearing that first successful boot-up - and we know not everyone wants to start from scratch. That's why we offer carefully assembled prebuilt systems designed to deliver solid performance right out of the box.
